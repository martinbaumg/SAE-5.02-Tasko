Your Ultimate Task Management Application

Tasko is more than just a simple to-do list. It's your all-in-one tool for efficient task management, designed to help you seamlessly organize your daily life. Whether you're a busy professional or a student looking to get organized, Tasko is here to simplify your task management.

Key Features

  • Intuitive Task Management

    Tasko provides a user-friendly interface for easily creating, organizing, and prioritizing your tasks. You can add, edit, and delete tasks in no time.

  • Time Management

    Precisely schedule your tasks by setting due dates and start times. Never miss important deadlines with customizable reminders.

  • Status, Tags, and Colors

    Categorize your tasks with custom status, tags, and color codes to indicate their importance and priority. Organize your tasks according to your own criteria.

  • Sign-In and Synchronization

    Create a Tasko account to access your task list from any device. Your data is synchronized in real-time, keeping you up to date no matter where you are.

  • Multi-Platform Availability

    • Tasko is compatible with Windows, Linux, and macOS, allowing you to use it on your desktop computer or laptop, as well as on your mobile device.

  • Data Security

    We understand the importance of your data security. Tasko employs advanced security protocols to ensure the confidentiality of your personal information.

  • Customization

    Tasko adapts to your work style. Customize the user interface to your preferences for an optimal user experience.
